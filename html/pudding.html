Pudding Time!

Ingredients

Caramel:

  • 1 Cup of Sugar
  • 1/4 cup of water

Custard:

  • 3 Whole Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup of Sugar
  • 2 Cups of Milk
  • 1 Tablespoon of Vanilla

instructions

In a pan combine sugar and water, make sure the sugar is evenly spread to prevent any burning. DONT STIR! Leave on medium/low heat until it reaches a beautiful golden color. Turn of the heat and allow to cool slightly then pour a few tablespoons into desired molds.

In another pan, heat milk and sugar in medium/low heat until fully dissolved, then add vanilla. In a seperate bowl, crack eggs and whisk until combined, slowly temper eggs with milk mixture until all is added. Strain mixture to prevent any cooked eggs from appearing in the final desssert. AVOID CHUNKY EGGS Pour milk mixture into molds but don't OVERFILL, leave about an inch from the top.

Cover the top of the molds with foil and place into a steamer over a simmering pot of water. Close the lid and steam for 10-13 minutes. Once steamed, let the molds cool room temp then chill in the fridge for about an hour. After being chilled remove the molds and serve!
