Jelly:

  • Gelatin
  • 1 1/2 Cup of Hot water
  • 3/4 Cup of Sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Lemon Extract
  • 1 Cup of Cold Water
  • Sliced strawberries

Cream:

  • 3 Egg Yolks
  • Sugar
  • Hot Milk
  • 2 teaspoons of Vanilla
  • Gelatin
  • Heavy Cream

First let's start with the jelly! In one bowl, bloom gelatin to activate, in a seperate bowl, mix hot water with sugar and mix. Make sure everything is dissolved and lemon extract. After the gelatin has melted add to our mixture. After adding the gelatin, add cold water to the bowl to help set the gelatin faster. Strain mixture if needed.

Get your strawberries ready as well as your molds. Fill molds halfway with gelatin mixture, then add the slices of strawberry. You can also add edible flowers for the aesthetic. Set jelly for at least 30 mins in the refrigerator.

To make the cream, you can use a stand/hand mixer and add three egg yolks and mix for a couple of seconds. Add sugar little by little on low speed, then add hot milk. Next add vanilla and gelatin. Remove from mixer and transfer mixture to stove.

In the stove you want to constantly mix to make sure all the sugar and gelatin is dissolved. We are looking for a very smooth, thick mixture, so we advise to strain it after you are done mixing. Now, we want to cool the hot cream, place mixture inside another bowl with ice. We want to lower the temp to room temp, CONSTANTLY STIR! After reaching room temp, add heavy cream and whip mixture with hand mixer. You are seeking for a very thick mixture and strain again.

Bring back the molds for the very last step. Pour cream onto set gelatin, don't overfill. Place in the refrigerator until its fully set. Can take a few hours!
