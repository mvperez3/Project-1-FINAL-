Crunchy Pork Cutlet

Ingredients

  • 4 Pork Chops
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/4 Cup of Flour
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Panko
  • French Fries

First prepare the meat, create incisions to avoid the pork chops from curling. Use the back of a knife to flatten the meat and with your hands reshape the meat. Next, season the pork chops with salt, black pepper and garlic powder.

Have the flour, eggs, and Panko ready in different containers. Coat the meat with flour first, make sure it's fully coated. Then coat again with the whisked eggs, and then coat with Panko.

Over medium heat, add oil and once it's hot carefully lower pork AWAY from you to avoid any burns. Fry on both sides till golden brown.

Either you can buy store bought frozen french fries, or make them in the air fryer. Honestly is up to you!
