The Legendary Omelette with Rice

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 Cup of cooked rice
  • 1/2 of an onion
  • 1 large mushroom
  • Ketchup
  • Chicken
  • Butter
  • Salt

First in a bowl, beat 3 eggs till they are fully mixed.On the side, slice your chicken into smaller pieces for this recipe.

Next, you want to dice your onion and slice your onion. Start to heat a pan, and add about a tablespoon of butter.

Now saute your chicken, onion, and sliced mushrooms, and DON'T FORGET TO SEASON YOUR FOOD! AND FOR THE LOVE OF GOD DON'T USE ONLY SALT!!! WHAT IS THE WRONG WITH YOU?!

Next add your cup of cooked rice, make sure to keep stirring and that everything is coming together. And lastly add ketchup, yes ketchup you read that right!

Then, on a seperate pan you want to cook 1 beaten egg, this will be the omelette. As the omelette is starting to cook, you want to add your chicken rice in the middle. Next, you want to fold the sides of the omelette towards the middle to seal in the chicken rice, and flip till it is cooked. And enjoy!
